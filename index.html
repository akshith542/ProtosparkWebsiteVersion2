Our Mission

Code Should Have No Borders

ProtoSpark was founded by high school students who believe every child — regardless of country, language, or background — deserves access to quality computer science education. We run free, virtual coding camps that are fun, accessible, and impactful.

🎯

Free & Accessible

100% free for every student, everywhere. We believe cost should never be a barrier to learning to code.

🌐

International Reach

We organize chapters country-by-country, led by local volunteers who bridge language and time-zone gaps.

🚀

Student-Led

Founded and run entirely by high schoolers — passionate about giving back and building real leadership experience.

💡

Hands-On Learning

Students build real interactive projects using Scratch — games, animations, stories — by the end of camp.

Curriculum Highlights

From Zero to Creator

Our curriculum is built around Scratch — MIT's free visual coding platform. Students start building on Day 1 with no prior experience required.

⚡

Events & Interaction

Use "when clicked" and "when key pressed" triggers to make projects interactive and responsive.

💨

Motion & Animation

Move, turn, glide, and bounce sprites around the stage with motion and animation blocks.

🏆

Capstone Project

Students combine everything they've learned to build a unique interactive project — a game, story, or animation — and present it on the final day.
